- **secao:** Desenvolvimento › Ágil não é correr (callout `:::atencao`)

- **prompt:** Split illustration. Left: a rigid waterfall staircase diagram with arrows going only downward, cracking at a point labeled with a change/lightning icon, grey tones. Right: a circular loop of arrows (plan-do-learn) flowing smoothly, absorbing a change icon, bright colors. Flat vector, no paragraph text, clear contrast rigid vs adaptive. 16:9.
